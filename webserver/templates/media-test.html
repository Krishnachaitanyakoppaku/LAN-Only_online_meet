Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Check browser address bar for camera/microphone icons
  2. Click any blocked icons and select "Allow"
  3. Refresh this page and try again
  4. Check browser settings for camera/microphone permissions
  5. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
  6. Ensure camera/microphone are not being used by other apps
